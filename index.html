About SagittariUS

Sagittarius. Aim is a accredit advertising agency at the forefront of the Sri Lankan advertising sector. Powered by country's most creative minds, the agency is perfectly equipped to manage large-scale advertising, social media marketing, and public relations initiatives.

The agency's success originates from its entire integration to please its clients, regardless of account size, by personally becoming involved with the client and ensuring that the client's goals and objectives are met in a cost effective manner.

In addition to its specialisation in "Above the Line" advertising, the agency's affiliate companies may provide outdoor advertising, point of sale, and strategic event management solutions.
